# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 2026**

## 1. Introduction

BuildBrainOS ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Service. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process your personal information in connection with our websites, mobile applications, and services (collectively, the "Service").

This Privacy Policy applies to all users, including general contractors, workers, subcontractors, government entities, and suppliers.

## 2. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide:
- **Account Information**: Email, password, name, phone number, company name, role
- **Identity Verification**: Government-issued ID, business registration, tax ID
- **Financial Information**: Bank account, payment method, billing address
- **Insurance Documents**: Worker's compensation, liability insurance certificates
- **Certifications**: Professional licenses, training certifications, background checks
- **Documentation**: Project photos, blueprints, contracts, RFIs
- **Communications**: Messages, support tickets, feedback

### Information Collected Automatically:
- **Device Information**: IP address, device type, operating system
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, time spent, clicks, searches
- **Location Data**: Geolocation (with permission) for job matching
- **Cookies & Tracking**: To improve functionality and personalization

### Third-Party Information:
- **Background Checks**: From Checkr, Sterling Talent Solutions
- **Credit Verification**: From Equifax, Experian, TransUnion
- **Business Verification**: From Middesk, Dun & Bradstreet
- **KYC/AML**: From Persona, SumSub

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- **Provide Services**: Process payments, match jobs, verify credentials
- **Safety & Compliance**: Detect fraud, prevent money laundering, verify licensing
- **Improve Platform**: Analyze usage, develop features, provide recommendations
- **Communications**: Send emails, notifications, updates (opt-out available)
- **Legal Obligations**: Comply with KYC/AML, tax reporting, subpoenas
- **Customer Support**: Resolve issues, respond to inquiries
- **Marketing**: Promote new features (with consent)

## 4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We share information with:

- **Service Providers**: Stripe, Dwolla, AWS, PostgreSQL, MongoDB (under data processing agreements)
- **KYC Providers**: Persona, SumSub (for identity verification)
- **Payment Processors**: Bank of America, Wells Fargo (for ACH processing)
- **Government**: When required by law (FINCEN for AML, IRS for 1099 reporting, OSHA for safety)
- **Law Enforcement**: In response to subpoenas or legal process
- **Other Users**: Project owners see contractor details; workers see job info
- **Analytics**: Google Analytics, Mixpanel (anonymized)

**We never** sell personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain information for the following periods:

- **Working Data**: Active projects, jobs, payments (retained while active)
- **Transaction Records**: 7 years (for tax & audit purposes)
- **Account Data**: 3 years after account closure (for chargeback/dispute resolution)
- **Audit Logs**: 2 years
- **BG Check Results**: 1 year (or as required by law)
- **Failed KYC Applications**: 1 year

Users can request data deletion, except where retention is required by law.

## 6. Your Rights (CCPA, GDPR)

**If you are a California resident (CCPA):**
- Right to access your personal information
- Right to delete personal information
- Right to opt-out of data sales
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising your rights

**If you are an EU resident (GDPR):**
- Right to access your personal information
- Right to rectification (correct inaccurate data)
- Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to restrict processing
- Right to data portability
- Right to object to processing
- Right to withdraw consent

To exercise these rights, email: privacy@buildbrain.io

## 7. Security

We implement the following security measures:

- **Encryption**: AES-256 at rest, TLS 1.3 in transit
- **Password Policy**: Minimum 8 characters, complexity requirements, forced updates every 90 days
- **Multi-Factor Authentication**: SMS, authenticator app, biometric
- **Access Controls**: Role-based access, least privilege permissions
- **Monitoring**: Real-time intrusion detection, log monitoring
- **Audits**: Annual penetration testing, SOC 2 Type II compliance
- **Backups**: Daily backups, encrypted, stored in separate region

**No method of transmission is 100% secure.** We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Payment Data Security

Payment information is processed through PCI-DSS Level 1 compliant providers:
- Stripe (card processing)
- Dwolla (ACH processing)
- Unit.co (BaaS)

**We never** store complete credit card numbers. Card tokens are stored in encrypted format.

## 9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we become aware of collection from a minor, we delete it immediately.

## 10. International Transfers

Your data may be transferred to, stored in, and processed in countries other than your country of residence (including the United States). These countries may have data protection laws different from your home country.

By using the Service, you consent to the international transfer of your information. We implement Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) to protect international transfers.

## 11. Third-Party Links

The Service may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

## 12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of material changes via email or prominent notice on the Service. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
